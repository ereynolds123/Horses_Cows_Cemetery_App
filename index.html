Horses, Cows and Cemeteries

A simple car game for the country

Rules

  1. A horse on your team's side of the car is worth ten points for your team.
  2. A cow on your opponent's side of the car is worth negative one point for your opponent.
  3. A cemetery on your opponent's side of the car is worth negative 100 points for your opponent.
  4. To add points, click on the icon associated with your team.
  5. Your game will save in the browser so that you don't have to leave the browser open for the whole game.
    You can simply return when ready to add points.
